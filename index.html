This schedule is designed to keep us moving, healthy, and consistent throughout the year. We have curated a mix of official races and relaxed nature walks to ensure there is something for everyone, regardless of fitness level.

🏁 Official Races (5Ks)

We have selected one major race almost every month. These are fun, high-energy events.

Action Item: Please use the provided links to register early before prices go up!

🌲 Group Hikes (Nature Walks)

In the weeks between races, we meet for self-organized group hikes.

  • Beginner Distance: Hikes are kept between 1.5 to 5 miles.
  • No "Hiking" Experience Needed: If you can walk a 5K, you can do these hikes!

⏰ Timing

Hikes: Consistently scheduled for Saturdays at 10:00 AM.

Races: Times vary (usually early Saturday or Sunday mornings).
