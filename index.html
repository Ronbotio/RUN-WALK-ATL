What to Expect

Our goal is consistency and community. Here is how our events typically run:

🕒 Arrival

Please arrive 15 minutes early for hikes to find parking and the group. For 5K races, follow the race director's instructions (usually 45-60 mins early).

👟 Gear

Comfortable walking or running shoes are required. For hikes, "Dirt/Gravel" paths may be uneven. "Paved" paths are stroller and wheelchair friendly.

💧 Hydration

Always bring your own water. Some race events provide water stations, but most of our community hikes do not.

🤝 Community

We move at a conversational pace. No one is left behind during hikes. We finish together!
